Directions

The workshop will take place in the Nucleus Building on the King’s Buildings campus of the University of Edinburgh.

How to get to Edinburgh?

You can find all the useful information on how to get to Edinburgh here.

How to get to the Nucleus?

The Nucleus Building is located in the middle of King’s Buildings campus in the southern part of Edinburgh. It is a 50-minute walk or 30 to 35 minutes on the bus from the city centre. The closest bus stops are Lady Road (bus 3, 7, 8, 29, 31, 37, 47, 49, X62), King’s Buildings (bus 9), West Mains Road (bus 38), Rankin Drive (bus 12, 24), Mid Liberton (7, 31, 37, 47). Google Maps is the most reliable app to know the real-time updated bus times.

The Nucleus Building

Elm Lecture Theatre

The Elm Lecture Theatre is on the ground floor of the Nucleus Building. You will find it at the back of the hallway (on your right) when entering through the main entrance.

Rowan Studio Classroom

The Rowan Studio Classroom is on the second floor of the Nucleus Building. You will find it on your left when coming from the stairway or the lift.
